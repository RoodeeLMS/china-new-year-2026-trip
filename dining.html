Restaurants & Food Experiences

Document your dining plans and food experiences in Hangzhou and throughout the trip.

Information to Include:

  • Restaurant names and addresses
  • Cuisine types and specialties
  • Reservation details and contact info
  • Estimated costs per meal
  • Operating hours and days closed
  • Transportation to restaurants
  • User reviews and ratings

Hangzhou Local Specialties to Try:

  • West Lake Fish in Vinegar Gravy: Famous Hangzhou dish
  • Dragon Well Tea: Premium green tea from the region
  • Hangzhou Roasted Duck: Local variation of Peking duck
  • Shrimp with Longjing Tea: Signature dish combining local ingredients
  • Bamboo Shoots & Chestnuts: Seasonal specialties
  • Local Snacks: Street food and market delicacies

Restaurant Categories:

  • Fine Dining: Upscale restaurants with traditional cuisine
  • Local Eateries: Authentic small restaurants and noodle shops
  • Street Food: Markets and vendor stalls
  • Hotel Dining: On-site restaurant options
  • International: Options for variety and comfort

Dining Tips:

  • Chinese New Year period may have altered hours
  • Make reservations in advance for popular restaurants
  • Cash (RMB) may be needed at smaller establishments
  • Bring translation app for menu reading
  • Tipping is not customary in mainland China
